# Thinking Journal

A daily practice space for sharpening critical thinking. Use this to:

- Reflect on decisions you made — were they rational?
- Notice biases in your own thinking
- Record interesting arguments you encountered
- Practice steelmanning opposing views
- Track your calibration over time

The journal is not about being right — it's about getting better at noticing when you might be wrong.

## Prompts
- What belief did I update today?
- Where did I catch myself using motivated reasoning?
- What argument did I dismiss too quickly?
- What would change my mind about a strongly held belief?
- What did I predict incorrectly? Why was I wrong?
