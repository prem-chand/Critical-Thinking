# Probability & Statistics

Probability and statistics give us the quantitative language for uncertainty — essential for reasoning about evidence, updating beliefs, and evaluating research claims. This thread moves from intuitive probability through Bayesian reasoning to sophisticated statistical critique.

## Spiral Progression

| Phase | Months | Depth | Focus |
|-------|--------|-------|-------|
| Foundation | 1—3 | Level 1 | Probability intuition, conditional probability, Bayes theorem basics |
| Application | 4—6 | Level 2 | Statistical claims, p-values, confidence intervals, effect sizes |
| Synthesis | 7—9 | Level 3 | Calibration, Bayesian reasoning in practice, statistical critique |
| Teaching | 10—12 | Level 4 | Research Audit Framework, Brier scores, teaching statistical reasoning |
