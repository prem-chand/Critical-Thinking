# Curriculum Hub — Critical Thinking for Researchers

A 12-month structured curriculum that teaches researchers critical thinking through an integrated lens of philosophy, logic, probability, statistics, scientific reasoning, and paper critique. The entire curriculum lives as a single Obsidian knowledge graph — a web of interlinked notes, exercises, templates, and structured claims that students build and inhabit throughout the year.

> **North Star:** By Month 12, a researcher can independently audit any research paper in their field using a structured framework integrating logic, probability, statistics, scientific reasoning, and metacognitive calibration — and can articulate their confidence with calibrated probabilistic estimates.
