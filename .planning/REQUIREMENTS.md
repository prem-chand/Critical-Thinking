# Requirements: Critical Thinking for Researchers — 12-Month Curriculum

**Defined:** 2026-06-14
**Core Value:** Transform a researcher's relationship with their own thinking — from passive consumer to self-calibrating reasoner who explicitly tracks beliefs, quantifies uncertainty, and catches cognitive errors before they become published mistakes.

## Out of Scope

| Feature | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| Custom Obsidian plugins | Leverage existing community plugins per Stack research |
| Video lectures or narrated content | Text-first, Obsidian-native only |
| Individualized tutoring or feedback | Curriculum is self-guided |
| Domain-specific stats (bioinformatics, econometrics) | Teach general principles; domain-bridge templates handle specificity |
| Web app or external database | Everything in-vault |
| Automated grading | Not possible in Obsidian; model answers enable self-check |
| Community/social features | Would require external platform |
