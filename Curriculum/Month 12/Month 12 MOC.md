# Month 12 — Final Research Audit

**Outcome:** Produce a polished, reusable Research Audit Framework (RAF) evaluation of a paper of the student's choice — demonstrating all 12 months of skills.

This is the **capstone peak** — the culmination of everything you've built. Reduced content load leaves mental space for the final audit. The resulting RAF workflow becomes a tool you can reuse on any paper going forward.

> [!success] You're in the final stretch.
> 95% of the work is behind you. This last 5% — synthesizing everything into a polished audit — is the most valuable part.

## Milestone: Final Research Audit (Capstone)

This is your independent demonstration of everything you've learned. Choose a paper — ideally from your own research area — and run a complete RAF evaluation:

- **Argument analysis** (Logic & Reasoning thread)
- **Statistical critique** (Probability & Statistics thread)
- **Method and replication audit** (Scientific Reasoning thread)
- **Belief audit and calibration report** (Metacognition & Calibration thread)

The output is a polished document you can reuse as a template for future paper evaluations.

After completing Week 3, generate your certificate from the `_templates/Certificate of Completion.md` template. This markdown certificate documents your journey and the skills you've mastered. Store it in the vault root as `My Certificate of Completion.md`.
