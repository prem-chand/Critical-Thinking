# Logic & Reasoning

Logic and reasoning form the backbone of critical thinking — the ability to parse arguments, evaluate their structure, distinguish valid inferences from fallacious ones, and reason soundly toward conclusions. This thread runs through all 12 months, progressively deepening from basic argument recognition to full audit integration.

## Spiral Progression

| Phase | Months | Depth | Focus |
|-------|--------|-------|-------|
| Foundation | 1—3 | Level 1 | Argument identification, validity, soundness, basic fallacies |
| Application | 4—6 | Level 2 | Fallacies in published papers, statistical reasoning, critique structure |
| Synthesis | 7—9 | Level 3 | Causal reasoning, counterfactuals, adversarial argument analysis |
| Teaching | 10—12 | Level 4 | Complete audit integration, peer review, teaching others to reason |
