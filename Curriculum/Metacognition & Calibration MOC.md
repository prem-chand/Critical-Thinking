# Metacognition & Calibration

Metacognition — thinking about thinking — is the meta-skill that makes all other critical thinking skills stick. This thread trains students to track their confidence, recognise their own biases, calibrate their predictions, and catch themselves before they rationalise poor reasoning. It is the thread that prevents critical thinking from becoming a tool for criticising others while remaining blind to oneself.

## Spiral Progression

| Phase | Months | Depth | Focus |
|-------|--------|-------|-------|
| Foundation | 1—3 | Level 1 | Confidence tracking, bias awareness, journaling habits |
| Application | 4—6 | Level 2 | Calibration practice, bias identification in self, prediction logs |
| Synthesis | 7—9 | Level 3 | Identity-protective cognition, motivated reasoning, debiasing |
| Teaching | 10—12 | Level 4 | Belief audit, Brier score history, teaching calibration to others |
