# Month 10 — First Paper Audit

**Outcome:** Complete a messy-first-draft paper audit that applies all six curriculum disciplines simultaneously — accepting imperfection as the goal.

This is the first **peak** milestone. The audit is intentionally rough — the goal is to experience applying everything at once. Month 11 will fill the gaps.

> [!tip] This is your first **peak** milestone.
> The goal is not a perfect audit — it's _experiencing_ the full workflow. Month 11 exists to fix what's rough. You cannot fail this month if you finish it.

## Milestone: First Complete Paper Audit

By the end of this month, you will have produced a complete (if messy) Research Audit Framework evaluation of a real paper in your field. This is the single most important output of the entire curriculum — everything prior has been building toward this.

- **Messy-by-design:** Expect your audit to have weak spots. The RAF is a tool you'll refine across the next two months.
- **Gap identification:** Every weakness you find becomes a learning target for Month 11's Gap Menu.
- **Celebrate the draft:** Imperfect completion beats perfect incompletion.
