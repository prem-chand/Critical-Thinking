**Output:** 4 Thread MOCs + 12 Monthly MOC stubs in `Curriculum/Month NN/` directories.

    4. **Week Structure** (4 weeks per month) — Table format:

       | Week | Topic | Core/Enrich | Lessons | Exercises | Paper |

       | 4 | [topic] | Core | — | — | — |
