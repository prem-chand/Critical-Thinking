# Denial

The refusal to accept reality or facts, often as a psychological defense mechanism to avoid uncomfortable truths. Denial can range from simple disbelief to complete rejection of evidence.

## Examples
- Rejecting scientific consensus on climate change despite overwhelming evidence
- Denying a personal health problem despite medical diagnosis
- Refusing to acknowledge a relationship is failing

## Why It Happens
Denial protects us from psychological pain, anxiety, and the need to make difficult changes. It allows us to maintain our current worldview and avoid confronting threatening information.

## How to Counteract
- Create safe spaces for honest conversation
- Gradually expose yourself to uncomfortable truths
- Seek support from trusted friends or professionals
- Focus on the long-term benefits of accepting reality
