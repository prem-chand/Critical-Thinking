# Month 11 — Gap-Filling & Review

**Outcome:** Consolidate weaker areas identified in the Month 10 messy audit and strengthen all curriculum disciplines before the capstone.

This is the **valley** — a lighter-paced month designed to prevent final-stretch dropout. You've earned the slowdown. Only 3 weeks, ~50% less content than a normal month. Select from the Gap Menu based on your Month 10 results.
